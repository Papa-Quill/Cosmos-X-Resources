//INFO//

This is a simple texture and model mod that replaces the default wrench with an golden variant.

//INSTALLATION//

step one: Open the "golden-wrench" folder

step two: drag and drop all of the files into your extracted root folder (zip0.360)

step three: Use VXZip to zip your modded root to your zip0.360 (or zip1.360 if you want)

step four: Profit
